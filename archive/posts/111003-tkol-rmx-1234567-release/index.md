Radiohead will celebrate the release of their remix album 'TKOL RMX 1234567' by streaming a live DJ set via <a class="bol" href="http://boilerroom.tv/" target="_blank">The Boiler Room</a> next Tuesday 11th October, from around 8pm UK time.<br/><br/>Thom will be DJing, along with Jamie XX, Caribou, Lone and Illum Sphere, who all feature on the album.<br/><br/>You can watch Radiohead's takeover of The Boiler Room live via <a class="bol" href="http://boilerroom.tv" target="_blank">www.boilerroom.tv</a> or <a class="bol" href="http://www.radiohead.com/deadairspace" target="_blank">www.radiohead.com/deadairspace</a>.<br/><br/>'TKOL RMX 1234567' is a CD compilation which collates all the 12" remixes that have been released throughout the summer. The album is released on October 10th and will be available from most music stores. <br/><br/>You can also pre-order it <a class="bol" href="http://www.thekingoflimbs.com/Store/DisplayItems.html" target="_blank">here</a>.
