Over the summer, Radiohead will be putting out a series of 12" vinyl releases, using remixes of tracks from The King Of Limbs.<br/><br/>The first release on the 4th July, starts with Little By Little by Caribou and Lotus Flower by Jacques Greene.<br/><br/>They will be available at selected independent record stores and from <a class="bol" href="http://www.radiohead.com" target="_blank">www.radiohead.com</a> via The King of Limbs tab.<br><br><br><br/></br></br></br>
