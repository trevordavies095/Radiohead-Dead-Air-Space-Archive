'<a class="bol" href="http://theislandpresident.com/" target="_blank">The Island President</a>' is a film about the Maldives, and the struggle of President Nasheed to get the voice of a small nation heard in the climate change debate.<br/>Unless something is done to stop  rising sea levels they will lose everything. <br/>The country will be under water.<br/>Some of our music was used to help tell the story.<br/>The film won 'Best Documentary' at the Toronto  film festival and will be screened this week at the Sundance film festival.<br/>
