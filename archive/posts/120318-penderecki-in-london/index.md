<a class="bol" href="http://www.barbican.org.uk/music/event-detail.asp?ID=12902" target="_blank">Penderecki is coming to London</a> this week, with the AUKSO Chamber Orchestra . They're going to play the music on the recording we've just <a class="bol" href="http://www.nonesuch.com/albums/threnody-for-the-victims-of-hiroshima-popcorn-superhet-receiver-polymorphia-48-responses-to-p" target="_blank">released</a> together. <br/><br/>To hear him conduct Threnody for the Victims of Hiroshima and Polymorphia with this wonderful Polish orchestra is a really magical experience, so if you're in London on Thursday, perhaps you'd like to come along.....<br/><br/>
